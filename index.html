    ASE Server Rules

    1. Harrassment/Griefing

    1a. No Excessive harassment, cursing, name calling, spamming in chat or other channels or Griefing. This definition can be found online or in our main rules section. You may foundation wipe caves for the intention of gaining that spot but not for the sole intention of creating malice toward another player or tribe. You may not foundation wipe non cave bases. You may not wipe all dinos.

    2. Characters

    2a. No naming your in game character 123, human, or the like. No false identities or aliases of any kind. If you fake your identity to the admins it is an immediate ban from ZLG.

    2b. Only 1 character per cluster; no duplicates (if I do see duplicates I will delete the first one I see).

    2c. No naming your character or tribe inappropriate names with curse words/slangs or words that incite illegal behavior such as drug usage/crimes/etc.

    3. Raids

    3a. Fobs - Can not have a MAGA ORP attached to them - Do not need to be removed after raid. Active raid fobs are off limits from 3rd party raiding. Once the raid is over they are fair game.

    3b. Team Raiding - One team is allowed to raid another tribe at a time. You can all fight each other for who GETS to raid but you can not team raid to take down a tribe together. This also includes defending and is considered teaming.

    3c. Killing Passives - No killing passives unless they would otherwise impede your raid. Killing dinos that are just there for breeding is not allowed. This only runs people off the cluster. They may be killed if you plan on taking the spot you are raiding. "Passive tames" is considered as breeding lines only.

    4. Tribes

    4a. The same tribe must remain on all servers under the same tribe name same owner. You are not allowed to tribe hop for PVP or alliances.

    4b. In the event a tribe is wiped they will have 4 days of raid protection from all tribes to get back on their feet.

    4c. If a tribe wants to replace or remove the owner for whatever reason there must be a recorded vote of 60% of members to pass. This can only take place once every 2 weeks.

    4d. You can not tribe hop. If you leave a tribe you can not rejoin that tribe for 2 weeks.

    5. Building

    5a. All structures must be viewable above ground and able to be destroyed.

    5b. No building on or in entrances to other biomes such as pathways on Gen2 space tunnels, Valguero tunnels, Aberration Surface entrances. *On Gen2 1 vent allowed per tribe.

    5c. No building in areas unattainable by walking there or flying or normal intended means. No meshing/mesh building.

    5d. No building in spawn points, nests, wyvern scars, mission zones, or banned locations (locations below).

    5e. No illegal racers, quetzals, or other illegally built platform dinos or skiffs. Transports must have at least two sides open to attack.

    5f. No building behind another tribe in a cave or structure. No building into enemy foundation range in order to block, build, bypass, destroy, or mesh into a base.

    5g. You are limited to one spot that a stego can not walk through per cluster. (IE You can not have a base in Center bear cave and crouch cave on Lost Island at the same time.)

    5h. Using a Fortnite to build up to or across to a base to better soak or get a cleaner angle to raid from is allowed.

    6. Ark Code of Conduct Rules

    6a. All rules on Ark CoC page apply with the exception of alliances. That is allowed. https://support.survivetheark.com/hc/en-us/articles/220278968-Code-of-Conduct

    6b. No Aimbots, or third party crosshairs or ESPs. This is bannable.

    6c. No changing of game files for any reason.

    7. Server wipe

    7a. Once a month after the initial 4 months I will call a vote for wipe or not. It must be 70% for the vote to pass. In the event the vote passes for a wipe it will take effect 1 week after the vote closes during which rule 3 is no longer valid until the wipe is completed.

    Zombie Lynx Disciplinary Actions

    Discipline is per Admin discretion - if you break any of these rules, the punishment is completely up to the staff. Punishments range all the way from a warning to a permanent ban depending on the offense.

  • 1st Offense: You will be issued a written warning and placed on probation for 10 days. You will also be issued a 1 day Discord server mute and 2 days if you do not respond to the disciplinary ticket.
  • 2nd Offense: You will be placed on a 2 day ban from all game servers and placed on probation for 30 days. This can not be appealed.
  • 3rd Offense: You will be placed on a 15 day ban from all ZLG servers and Discord. You have 3 days to appeal this decision in writing while being timed out in Discord before the ban takes place. The admins have 24 hours to reply to the appeal.
  • 4th Offense: In the event you return after the 15 day ban or your appeal is stricken and you have another offense, you will be permanently banned (perma-banned) from ZLG and all of its services. All digital currency will not be refunded or returned.

    • -----This list is subject to change at any time without notice. Breaking these rules is enforceable listed under "Zombie Lynx Ark Disciplinary Actions"

    Zombie Lynx Ark Cluster Support

    1. Character Loss

    1a. If your character is deleted you must provide an image of your implant with level and ascensions/bosses to have these restored. This photo must have a timestamp at the bottom of the screen and also show the server we are on as well. You can do this by pressing escape in game. I suggest taking a screenshot after any ascension fight with time and date at bottom of your screen (you can do this by going out of fullscreen mode). Crashes/bugs happen. WE don't always have control of it. This is why you are expected to record what you are doing/have on you at all times.

    2. Dino/Item/Boss Issues

    2a. In the event of a dino loss/boss glitch/etc you must provide evidence of the event that made your dino get lost (there are many game recording softwares out there. Nvidia comes built in as does AMD).

    3. Server Outages

    3a. For server outages you can try the many solutions we have posted in the discord under #ark-faq and then if that still is not helping you may file a ticket.
